About NewBee

NewBee is an iOS app developed with 'newbee' beekeepers in mind. Never done it before, but want to give it a try? Welcome! Don't know anyone to ask questions? NewBee is here for you!

Resources

Bee MD is your Web MD for bees! Caring for a macro-organism is different than caring for a cat or a dog. Observe your bees, and if anything seems off, checkout the Symptoms page. You might find some underlying causes, which are voted based on likliness of the connection. Causes connect you to potentially helpful Treatments. Please vote responsibly, and remember that bees' lives are at stake!

Monitor

Using "Internet of Things" (or, as I call it,Internet of Stings), NewBee uses a Raspberry Pi device to monitor the hive. It grabs temperature and humidity data and sends it back to you so you can be rest assured they don't get too hot in the summer, or too cold in the winter.

Inspections

Instead of taking your pet for a walk, a beekeeper's duties is primarily doing hive Inspections. These inspections are designed to help a newbee know what to look for, to remember what happened last time you checked in on them, and help you track their progress and well-beeing.
